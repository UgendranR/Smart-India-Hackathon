Safety Measures

All miners should know the coal mining safety measures.

Coal mining is one of the most dangerous industries in the world. Human factors such as intentional violation, mismanagement, and defective design cause coal mining accidents

The functional climate of the coal mining presents many hazards to personnel, including the proximity to machinery, hydraulic and electrical power, roof falls, and exposure to explosive mine gases and dust. Coalners are often required to work in this unsafe climate to physically control gear at short proximity and subsequently accomplish productive extraction
